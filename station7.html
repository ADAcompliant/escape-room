It will flag overdue actions for any crew member on the sepsis pathway — but only if you know how to interpret its alerts and respond correctly. Without it, crew members showing early signs of sepsis could deteriorate quickly!

Your mission: work through each Medical Bay section, interpret the clinical situation correctly, and take the right action to increase the lives you've saved!
